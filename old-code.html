Hi, I'm Rob, It's nice to meet you!

I am a web developer and graphic designer based out of the New York City area.

I am passionate about the design and development of simple, yet beautiful apps from concept to finish. I value clean compositions with just enough character to tell their own story.

I constantly push myself to visualize ideas from a unique perspective, and enjoy dreaming up creative solutions to bring them to life.

When I'm not coding, you'll usually find me having fun outdoors. I enjoy going on hikes, taking photos, and am always down for a round of golf. I also love learning new things, and am a firm subscriber to the philosophy that we are always students.
